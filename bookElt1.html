A security deposit of $1000.00* is required prior to RV pickup. Included in your trip are 500 mi and 20 generator hours. $0.35/mi and $3/hr will be charged for excess mileage and hours generatour hours.

    > $100 – Dumping Fee If gray and black water tanks are not fully dumped upon return, this fee will be assessed

    > $100 – Early Pick-up Fee Pick up is after 3PM - if you need it earlier, this fee will be assessed.

    > $200 – Excess cleaning fee This fee will be assessed if the RV is returned excessively dirty (i.e. mud, stains, trash)

    > $150 per night – Festival Fee Additional fee per night for all festivals

    > $25 – Fuel Fill $25 fee plus cost of fuel if not returned with same fuel level as departure

    > $100 – Late Return Fee Drop off is by 3:00pm - if you need to drop it off later than that, this fee will be assessed.

    > $350 – Smoking Fee Absolutely no smoking in the rig. If there is evidence of smoking or a smokey smell, this fee will be assessed.
